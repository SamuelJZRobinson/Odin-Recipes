Chicken Florentine

Creamy and savory Chicken Florentine, a delectable dish featuring tender chicken, spinach, and a rich Parmesan cream sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 teaspoon kosher salt, divided
  • 2 (10-oz. each) boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut in half horizontally
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • 3/4 cup chicken stock
  • 3/4 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/8 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/8 teaspoon dried marjoram
  • 1 (5-oz.) package fresh baby spinach (about 4 cups packed)
  • 1/4 cup roughly chopped fresh basil
  • 1 ounce Parmesan cheese, finely shredded (about 1/4 cup)

Steps

  1. Coat chicken cutlets with flour and 1/4 tsp salt; set aside.
  2. Brown chicken in butter and oil until cooked through; transfer to a wire rack.
  3. Reduce wine in skillet, then add stock, cream, and seasonings; simmer until thickened.
  4. Add spinach; cook until wilted.
  5. Return chicken to skillet; heat through.
  6. Remove from heat; stir in basil and Parmesan.
