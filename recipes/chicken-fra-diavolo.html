Chicken Fra Diavolo

Source

Spicy and flavorful Chicken Fra Diavolo, a classic Italian dish featuring tender chicken in a bold, fiery tomato and herb sauce.

Ingredients

  • 6 skin-on, bone-in chicken thighs
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 hot red pepper, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes, or to taste
  • 1/2 cup white wine, such as Pinot Grigio
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 (14 1/2 ounce) crushed fire-roasted tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon minced fresh basil, plus more for garnish
  • 1 tablespoon fresh parsley
  • 2 tablespoons shredded Parmesan cheese, or to taste

  1. Preheat the oven to 375째F (190째C).
  2. Pat dry and season chicken thighs with salt and pepper.
  3. In an oven-safe skillet, heat olive oil and butter.
  4. Brown chicken thighs in batches; remove to a plate and keep warm.
  5. Cook onions, red pepper, and garlic in the skillet; add Italian seasoning and red pepper flakes.
  6. Stir in wine, tomato paste, and crushed tomatoes; add basil and simmer until slightly thickened.
  7. Nestle browned chicken thighs into the sauce, skin side up.
  8. Bake in the oven until the internal temperature reaches 165째F (74째C).
  9. Sprinkle with parsley, garnish with basil, and top with Parmesan cheese. Serve warm.
